We're excited to introduce **Gainbase** - your comprehensive portfolio tracking companion for iOS! Built with a focus on privacy, powerful analytics, and beautiful design, Gainbase helps you take complete control of your investment journey.

## üéØ What's Included

### Portfolio Management
- **Real-time Portfolio Tracking**: Monitor your investments with live price updates that refresh automatically every 5 minutes
- **Multi-broker Support**: Seamlessly track holdings across Upstox, Groww, IND Money, and other brokers in one unified view
- **Privacy Mode**: Instantly hide sensitive financial data with a single tap - perfect for sharing your screen or viewing in public
- **Comprehensive Metrics**: Track total value, daily returns, total returns, realized/unrealized P&L, invested amount, and XIRR

### Advanced Analytics
- **Multi-dimensional Analysis**: Visualize your portfolio breakdown by:
  - Sector distribution (IT, Banking, FMCG, etc.)
  - Individual companies with company logos
  - Asset types (Large Cap, Mid Cap, Small Cap, ETFs)
  - Broker allocation
- **Interactive Charts**: Beautiful pie charts with customizable views
- **Flexible Sorting**: Sort by current value, returns, or portfolio contribution
- **Deep Insights**: Tap on any company to view detailed stock-level analysis

### Performance Insights
- **Top Movers**: Instantly identify your best and worst performing holdings
- **Win/Loss Analysis**: Visual breakdown of winning vs losing positions with win rate metrics
- **Activity Calendar**: Heat map visualization of your trading activity patterns
- **Yearly Analysis**: Year-over-year performance tracking with expandable asset distribution
- **Monthly Analysis**: Month-by-month investment tracking and growth metrics
- **Portfolio Forecasting**: Project your portfolio's future value based on historical performance with configurable timeframes

### Transaction Management
- **Intuitive Transaction Entry**: Add buy/sell transactions with a clean, user-friendly interface
- **CSV/Excel Import**: Bulk import transactions from spreadsheets - no manual entry required
- **Complete History**: Full audit trail of all portfolio activities
- **Smart Data Validation**: Automatic error checking to ensure data accuracy

### Sharing & Customization
- **Shareable Performance Cards**: Generate beautiful, branded cards showcasing your portfolio performance to share on social media
- **Custom Branding**: Add your own logo and external links to personalize the experience
- **Currency Toggle**: Show or hide currency symbols based on your preference
- **Dark/Light Mode**: Automatic theme switching based on system preferences
